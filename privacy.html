Privacy Policy

The Standard · Last updated: May 2026

This privacy policy describes how The Standard ("the app") handles your data. The Standard is a personal training tracker built for individual use.

Data We Collect

  • Daily health metrics you enter manually (weight, sleep, recovery, nutrition)
  • Workout logs and notes you enter
  • WHOOP integration data (recovery, HRV, sleep) if you choose to connect your WHOOP account

How Your Data Is Used

  • All data is used solely to display your personal training dashboard
  • Data is stored in your browser's localStorage and optionally synced to Supabase (a third-party database) for cross-device access
  • Your data is never sold, shared with third parties, or used for advertising
  • No analytics, tracking scripts, or third-party cookies are used

WHOOP Integration

  • If you connect your WHOOP account, the app requests read-only access to your recovery, sleep, and body measurement data
  • Your WHOOP access token is stored only in your browser's localStorage
  • WHOOP data is fetched on demand and stored alongside your other training data
  • You can disconnect WHOOP at any time by clearing your browser data or revoking access in your WHOOP account settings

Data Storage

  • localStorage: Data is stored locally in your browser on your device
  • Supabase: If sync is enabled, data is stored in a private Supabase database accessible only to you
  • No one else has access to your data

Your Rights

  • You can delete all local data at any time by clearing your browser's localStorage
  • You can delete all synced data by running DELETE queries in your Supabase dashboard

Contact

This is a personal app. For any questions, contact the app owner directly.
